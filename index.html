ABOUT US

The ECE Society is the official society of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, BIT Sindri. The society aims to promote scientific thinking among the students and provide a holistic environment for students where the can freely explore the latest cutting-edge technologies in the field of Electronics and software solutions to the real-world problems.

The Society also organises various webinars and Alumni Talks where students learn from the experience of our distinguished Alumni working in diverse fields, ranging from Indian Administration to tech giants like Google, Amazon etc.

Our Activities

The society aims at enhancing the technical knowledge of students by conducting various programs like organising expert lectures, workshops, seminars, industrial visits, intra and inter-department technical competitions and bring out the passion, intelligence & creativity in them for bringing down the gap between industry and academics. It also helps to create the ability to understand and effectively communicate engineering and related concepts, nurture entreprenuership and leadership qualities to enhance their roles in providing service to society.
